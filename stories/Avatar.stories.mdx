An Avatar is a component used to display a user's profile picture. It comes in two sizes, `large` and `small`

<Canvas>
  <Avatar picture={avatar} size="large" />
  <Avatar picture={avatar} size="small" />
</Canvas>

It also gracefully handles missing pictures

<Canvas>
  <Avatar picture={null} size="large" />
  <Avatar picture={null} size="small" />
</Canvas>
